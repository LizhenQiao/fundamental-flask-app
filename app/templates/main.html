Readme

User login is the entrance of normal users. You would be asked to input your username and password to login. After login, you can access all the functions by clicking the navigation bar at the top of the page.
- Upload: you could upload images either by typing in an accessable URL or choosing from your local files.
- Album: you could view all your images and related images transformed here.
- Change Password: you could change password of the account you are currently login.
- Logout: you could logout the account and redirect to the entrance page by clicking Logout.

Admin login is the entrance of admins. You would be asked to input the username and password to login. After login, you can access all the functions by clicking the navigation bar at the top of the page.
- Register: you could add new accounts of normal users here by typing in Username, Password and Email
- Edit&Delete: you could delete or edit the name and password of normal user accounts here.
- Logout: you could logout the account and redirect to the entrance page by clicking Logout.

If you cannot recall your password, please click PasswordRecovery You would be asked to input the username of your account. An email will be sent to the email address attached to your account for you to reset the password.
